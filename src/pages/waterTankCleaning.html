Water Tank Cleaning

What’s Included and Excluded

Included

  • Drainage & Debris Removal

    Draining the water and removing sludge, dirt, and debris from the tank

  • Scrubbing & Sludge Removal

    Scrubbing interior walls and floor to eliminate algae, mud, and deposits

  • High-Pressure Cleaning

    Using high-pressure water jets to clean and rinse the tank thoroughly

  • Disinfection & Sanitization

    Applying safe disinfectants to eliminate bacteria, germs, and harmful microorganisms

  • Final Rinsing & Drying

    Flushing the tank with clean water and ensuring it is ready for refilling

Excluded

  • Pipeline Cleaning

    Internal cleaning of supply pipes or plumbing lines

  • Repair & Maintenance

    Fixing leaks, cracks, or replacing damaged parts of the tank

  • Underground Tank Cleaning

    Cleaning tanks located deep underground without proper access

  • Water Refilling

    Refilling the tank with fresh water after cleaning

  • Pest & Mold Removal

    Eliminating pests, rodents, or mold infestations inside the tank
